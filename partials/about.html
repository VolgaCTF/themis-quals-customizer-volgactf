Greetings everybody and welcome to VolgaCTF 2016 Quals!

To take part in the competition, you should sign up with email, team name and logo. Warning! You won't be able to change the team name so please be careful in order to avoid conflicts with other teams. The number of team members is not limited.

Please specify a valid email address to get verification email. It will be impossible to change the email adress after you confirm your participation by clicking the link in the verification email.

Also, we ask you to specify your country in the registration form - we are going to collect this information for our statistics.

Russian teams are kindly advised to fill institution field.

The contest starts on March 25 at 19:00 SAMT/15:00 UTC and lasts for 48 hours. During the competition we will add new tasks. Check the News page periodically - there might be some important pieces of information about the competition. We'll also provide you with a link to Telegram channel before the start of the competition in order to answer your questions and make other announcements.

Teams are forbidden to perform destructive actions against competition infrastructure (*.volgactf.ru and *.volgactf.org) and prevent other teams to play. A team may be disqualified for breaking the rules.

Top teams will be invited to take part in VolgaCTF 2016 Finals which will be held on September 12-16 in Samara, Russia. We will invite teams from higher educational institutions: 5 teams from the Volga Federal District, 4 teams from the other regions of Russian Federation and 3 foreign teams (hopefully). We have another restriction - only one team from one higher educational institution.

We reserve the right to amend these regulations, order of tasks opened and the final round regulations.
